### What?
We want to strengthen, inspire, and engage the young members of the Polar community. 

The total solar eclipse gives space to learn, think, and reflect. This is a philosophical, artistic, outdoor, and natural science workshop. Participants can connect with nature and, through nature, with each other. The workshop aims to strengthen relationships and collaboration among the young (“early-career”) polar community across borders. When datasets, funding, jobs, and subsequently a future are disappearing for a large part of the Polar community, it is time to strengthen our bonds and build resilience.
